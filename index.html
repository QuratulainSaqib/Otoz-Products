FAQ

Common questions

Which product should I sign into?

Staff use Otoz Flow for internal tasks, dealers and agents use Otoz Connect to track purchases, and yard or operations teams use Yard Master. Buyers browsing listings use the main Otoz app.

Do I need separate accounts for each product?

No — your Otoz account works across all the products you've been given access to. What you see depends on your role.

I don't see a product I should have access to.

Reach out to your account administrator to have the right role added to your login.

Is Yard Master the same as Otoz Connect?

No. Yard Master is the internal operations view of the yard and containers; Otoz Connect is what dealers see for their own vehicles. Yard Master tracks the floor, Connect tracks the order.
